The mouse said "Sorry mighty lion,please leave me I promise one day I will help you". The lion laughed "You a little mouse will help me, Ok let's see".And he leaved the mouse. One day a hunter came in the jungle he saw that the lion is sleeping so he putted the net on the lion and caught him.The lion roared and roared but no one came.Mouse heard the lion's roar and he thought that the lion must be in the trouble.He hurried up and when he came there he saw that the lion is covered with net.The mouse called his friends and started to cut the net by his mouths the all mouses cutted the net and made the lion free.The lion thanked mouse fr the help. And frm then they both became friends.

Moral of the story: A friend ineed is a friend indeed
